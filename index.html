Water Contraption

It's been a warm summer in Sunnyvale. I’ve been playing with reusable water balloons at the park. This requires large buckets of water and the park does not have a spigot.

I built a contraption out of spare parts to funnel water from a drinking fountain to the buckets. My materials?

  • A piece of packing styrofoam.
  • A pool noodle.
  • Two waterbottles.
  • Duct tape.
